VISA INTERNATIONAL SERVICE ASSOCIATION

VISA International Service Association is a system allows to pay internationally. Currently, the partnership includes two companies (previously there were four): Visa Inc. (USA, Foster City), which owns all trademark rights plus applicable technology, plus Visa Europe Services Inc. (UK, London), that is operated by European banks and operates under using of Visa Inc. licenses As for the total turnover on Visa cards, it is 8.3 trillion US dollars. Visa cards are accepted at points of sale in more than 200 countries. The organization plays a central role in the development of innovative payment products and technologies that are used by 15,500 financial institutions (as of March 31, 2019). There are 3.4 billion Visa cards as of December 31, 2018, which are accepted for payment at more than 53.9 million retail outlets in the world. The basis of the payment system is VisaNet - a global innovative processing network that processes over 65,000 transactions per second.

You could get strong purchase protections and travel insurance.

The VISA payment system allows the store to make payments for Visa cards using card information (card number, card expiration date, security code cvv2, in some cases the name of the card holder and his mailing address), without the need to confirm payment with an additional verification code (3-D Secure technology ) This approach to payment implies that in the event of a dispute regarding this purchase, the store will assume the risks associated with the legality of payment and the funds will be returned to the card holder. Thus, a store that supports 3-D Secure technology has a choice - to additionally verify the purchase using a verification code, reducing the risk of possible fraud, or not to use this verification, taking on the risks associated with possible fraud. The cvv2 code (the number is printed on the back of the plastic card) is forbidden to be stored at any stage of the purchase by any of the payment system participants. Thus, the risk of subsequent fraudulent purchases is significantly reduced if the saved payment details of the cards are compromised, since cvv2 is simply not stored anywhere. This security measure makes sure that the buyer has physical access to the card with every purchase.

MASTERCARD INCORPORATED

MasterCard is an international bank card of the same name payment system operating in 210 countries (more than 29 million points of reception). Another brand of this system is Maestro. The system was founded in 1966 - several American banks decided to create the Interbank Card Association. The modern name has existed since 1979. In 2015, MasterCard occupies 20% of the payment market. Over 23 billion transactions take place in a year. The Shakespeare question “Which is better - Visa or Mastercard?” Does not imply a definite answer. Both payment systems are international. However, the main currency of Visa is the US dollar, and MasterCard is both the dollar and the euro. In order not to lose money on currency conversion, in Europe it is better to use a Mastercard card, in the USA - prefer a Visa.

Visa vs MasterCard: What's the Difference?

Coverage of countries: 200 - for Visa versus 210 - for Mastercard.
  • Prevalence: Visa is widespread throughout the world than MasterCard - 29% of all cards belong to the first system, 16% to the second system.
  • Prevalence in Russia: Visa has more than 80 partner banks in our country, its share is 45%, and Mastercard has about 100 partner banks, while its share is estimated at 49%.
  • Payment options: Visa cards are accepted at more than 20 million companies worldwide. The MasterCard has this figure even higher - 30 million outlets.
  • Shopping on the Internet: both systems make it possible to securely pay for goods and services online.
  • Security: cards of both payment systems have comparable security settings. Visa uses the Visa Money Transfer service (to transfer money from a Visa card to a Visa card and to transfer cash to a Visa card through an ATM / terminal). A similar service, Mastercard MoneySend, is also used by a number of banks, while it is less common. In addition, Visa supports the optional “Verified by Visa” security system.
  • Distinctive features: in addition to the usual format cards, Visa and Mastercard also issue mini-cards (for example, Alfa-Bank customers who have issued a debit or credit Cosmo card receive a small key card in addition to the regular card). Its functionality is the same as that of the usual one, with the only difference being that the mini-card cannot be used in ATMs.
  • Special offers: the Visa system in Russia has about 50 partner companies, where cardholders can always get discounts of 5-10%, temporary promotions are regularly held. Mastercard has a Mastercard Rewards bonus program - by paying for purchases with a Mastercard, the holder receives bonus points and can exchange them for prizes from the program catalog - currently there are more than 200 different gifts in it. In addition, there are discount programs - however, there are not so many of them. In fact, it is safe to say that both systems are good, convenient and safe.
